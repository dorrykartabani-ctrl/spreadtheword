Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Go Spread The Word collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your chosen nickname (optional)
  • Your country (optional)
  • Your sharing activity within the app
  • Your platform preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Go Spread The Word service
  • Track your sharing impact and warrior progress
  • Display community statistics (anonymised)
  • Send daily quote notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve our service and user experience

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information. Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymised, aggregated data (such as total community shares) publicly to demonstrate the mission's impact.

5. Your Sharing Activity

When you share a quote through the app, we log the sharing action to track your personal impact stats. We do not access your social media accounts or post on your behalf. All sharing is initiated by you through your device's native share functionality.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage and session storage to maintain your login session and editor preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics trackers.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of notifications at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Go Spread The Word is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Subscription & Payment Data

Subscription payments are processed through secure third-party payment processors. We do not store your credit card details directly. Payment processing is handled in accordance with PCI-DSS standards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

support@gospreadtheword.app
